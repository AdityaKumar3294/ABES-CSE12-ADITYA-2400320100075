AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is a branch of computer science focused on creating machines or software that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. These tasks include things like: Learning from data Understanding language Recognizing images and sounds Making decisions Solving problems There are different types of AI: Narrow AI: Designed to do one specific task (like virtual assistants or recommendation systems). General AI: A theoretical AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can (this doesn't exist yet). In short, AI is about teaching computers to think and learn in ways similar to humans.
